Frequently Asked Questions

What sizes are available?

We offer both kids and adults sizes: XS, S, M, L. Check each product for exact availability.

How long does shipping take?

Processing takes 1–3 business days. Delivery typically takes 3–7 business days within the U.S.

Can I return or exchange an item?

Yes! Returns and exchanges are accepted within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unused and in original condition.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only ship within the United States. International shipping may be added in the future.

How do I contact customer support?

Email us at support@innocentclothing.com for any questions or issues.
